What we write about

MonoBlog is a small corner of the internet that talks about three things I care about: code, design, and anime. Light reads, simple ideas, and a bit of nerd energy.

Programming It teaches you how to think.

Programming is more than just typing code. It’s understanding a problem, breaking it into smaller parts, and building something that actually works. Posts here focus on simple explanations, small projects, and the mindset behind learning to code without burning out.

Design Play with your imagination.

Design is how ideas are translated into something people can see, touch, and use. Here the focus is on simple layouts, sketching, and learning to notice details — contrast, spacing, and composition — without getting lost in jargon or tools.

Anime Stories that stay with you.

Anime mixes bold visuals with emotional storytelling. Posts in this section talk about shows, characters, and moments that hit hard, whether it’s a silly slice-of-life episode or an overpowered hero saving the world again.
